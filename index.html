Balanced Diet

Balanced Diet

A balanced diet means eating a variety of foods in the right amounts so your body gets all the energy and nutrients it needs to stay healthy.

  • Carbohydrates and fats
  • Vitamins
  • Minerals
  • Fibre
  • Water

Micronutrients

Micronutrients are nutrients that the body needs in small amounts but are very important for health.

  1. Iron
  2. Calcium
  3. Potassium
  4. Zinc
  5. Magnesium

Food, Drink, and Health

The food and drink we choose every day have a big effect on our health. Eating fresh fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lean proteins, and drinking enough water helps keep our body strong and healthy.
